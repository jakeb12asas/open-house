🇳🇵 Nepal Landmark Quiz

How to Play:

  • Enter your name and select a difficulty on the next page.
  • Each round shows a pixelated landmark image.
  • Click the correct name from 4 options.
  • Correct answers give +100 points.
  • Wrong answers show ❌, try again if time allows.
  • After all rounds, your score is saved to the leaderboard.

Have fun exploring Nepal’s beautiful landmarks! 🇳🇵
